Why It Matters

Outside companies influence people standing inside this community.

Right now, outside companies decide where guests eat, shop, and book next. They don't have to be here. They don't have to contribute.

This system changes that.

  • Supports local businesses first
  • Keeps revenue inside the community
  • Creates a more consistent visitor experience

Protection + Control

This isn't just advertising. It's control over the digital environment.

  • Reduces outside influence from national chains
  • Limits aggressive retargeting and tracking
  • Aligns what people see with the community around them
  • Fast alert system for emergencies
  • Protects our families and guests against outside intruders

Over time, this becomes a new economic layer — where local businesses can own access to attention here.

A Cleaner Digital Environment

Today, attention inside the community is sold to the highest bidder.

The most aggressive advertiser — not the most trusted business — often wins attention.

The perimeter shifts that.

  • Reduces unwanted retargeting from outside companies
  • Limits misleading or high-pressure advertising
  • Creates a more predictable, trusted digital experience

Not restriction — just a cleaner, more intentional signal.

The Carlton Landing digital perimeter is currently live and operating.

The objective is simple: prove that physical places can own and operate their digital environment.
